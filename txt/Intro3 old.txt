She scratches her elbows. "Ah, and before you  start, I have something for
you." She begins rummaging behind her armchair, muttering to herself. She
emerges shortly with a small wicker picnic basket.

***

"Take this. You may use the items within as you wish for your negotiations. You
can trade these away for the keys, but I can't guarantee that everyone will
want everything. However, this is all you get: it must last you all three
negotiations. Whatever you end with is yours to keep. Understand? Good." She
hands over the basket.
